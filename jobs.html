Volunteering

As you can see, we work hard, there are tasks to complete and for that we always welcome all the help we can get. You can be part of our group of volunteers. There is always something you can do to aid our cause. If you are interested in this opportunity, send us a message at info@shellseaproject.org

Scholarship Program

The principal goals of this program are:

  • To generate educational opportunities.
  • To remove economic barriers that may block access to a better science education.
  • To mentor students in biology, conservation, and environmental education.
